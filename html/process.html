The Process



The process of accomplishing this mission begins by providing a curriculum that focuses on a variety of personal life skills training programs along with a number of community oriented service and team building programs that include the following:

PERSONAL LIFE SKILLS TRAINING: These skills will be taught through a series of participatory educational videos and personal life skills training programs that have been donated by the Anthony Robbins Foundation. The main course will run approximately 4-6 weeks and be presented by experienced and qualified Life Coaches.

PROBLEM SOLVING & STRATEGIC PLANNING: Teaching youth how to identify and address different concerns they have and how to employ certain strategies in order to overcome those concerns and help accomplish their goals.

LOCAL GOVERNMENT PARTICIPATION: Students will learn how local government works and how they can get involved with local government in order to help impact the decisions and policies that affect themselves and their community.

MENTORING: The Academy will involve local community leaders, local politicians, area athletes and other personal success stories from local residents in order to inspire and motivate the young adults to create their own success stories.

COMMUNITY SERVICE PROJECTS: Students will be able to participate in a variety of local community service programs that will have a positive and direct impact on their local communities and neighborhoods.

ACADEMIC ASSISTANCE: One of the main academic goals of the Academy is to be in a financial position to offer students financial assistance with their higher academic career goals, college and or special assistance with their basic high school homework.

FIELD TRIPS: The Academy will feature a variety of field trips that seek to educate, motivate and inspire team building among the students.
