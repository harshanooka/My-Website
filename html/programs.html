INITIAL PILOT PROGRAM

One of the courses designed to run several times per year is a life skills and personal strategies program that was donated by the Tony Robbins Foundation.

This course is taught by a skilled Life Coach, trained specifically in these programs.

POLITICAL ADVOCACY
Students will be able to participate in leadership programs that show how their local government works.

Participants will also learn how they can get involved in the political process to support and or affect change they want to see happen in their community.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

Students will be able to participate in local community events, helping to improve their neighborhoods and their city, while obtaining a sense of personal accomplishment that only comes from taking action.
