CAMDEN AND THE REALITIES FOR ITS YOUTH

Camden has been ranked repeatedly as one of the most dangerous and violent cities in the US.

Many young people become involved in crime and the criminal justice system at an early age.

MANY YOUTH HAVE BEEN EXPOSED TO POVERTY CRIME & VIOLENCE THROUGHOUT THEIR LIVES

CAMDEN IS ONE OF THE MOST IMPOVERISHED CITIES IN THE NATION

The High School Dropout Rate is approximately 60%.
Many families are single-parent led and run mostly by a grandparent or young mother.
The Unemployment Rate is 3 times the national average.

In one particular survey, the #1 request by city youth was for a “safe” place to play. Enough food to eat was the second request
